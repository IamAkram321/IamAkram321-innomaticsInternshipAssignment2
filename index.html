About Us

Brewed Awakening is a local, family-owned coffee shop that has been serving up exceptional coffee since 2010. Our mission is to provide our customers with the best coffee they can find, and to make that coffee a joy to enjoy.

We believe in creating a connection between people and coffee, and we strive to make every cup a special experience. We invite you to join us at our events and enjoy our coffee with your loved ones.
